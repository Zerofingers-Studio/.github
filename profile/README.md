### About Us
We’re zerofingers, an independent game development studio in Thessaloniki, Greece.
Our fingers are our skills, that's why always ours team total fingers are always count down to zero.
We are gamers and huge injoyers of Rust game, one of the most popular FPS survivors games in Steam.
Multiplayer games is a no brainer for us, as we love the players to interact with each other as a team or as enemies.
Unfortunately we still getting familiarised with the game development process and for sure we should start with something simplier.
We love working on games from ground up, from the concept to the final product.
You will hear from us soon!
